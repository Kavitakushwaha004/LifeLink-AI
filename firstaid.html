🩹 First Aid Guide

❤️ CPR

1. Check if the person is responsive.
2. Call 108 immediately.
3. Give 30 chest compressions.
4. Give 2 rescue breaths.
5. Continue until help arrives.

🔥 Burn Injury

1. Cool the burn with running water for 20 minutes.
2. Do not apply toothpaste or oil.
3. Cover with a clean cloth.
4. Visit the nearest hospital.

🩸 Heavy Bleeding

1. Apply direct pressure using a clean cloth.
2. Raise the injured area if possible.
3. Do not remove soaked cloth.
4. Call emergency services.

🦴 Fracture

1. Do not move the injured part.
2. Use a splint if available.
3. Apply ice using a cloth.
4. Reach the hospital immediately.

🐍 Snake Bite

1. Keep the patient calm.
2. Do not cut or suck the wound.
3. Remove tight jewellery.
4. Take the patient to hospital immediately.

⚡ Electric Shock

1. Switch off the electricity supply.
2. Don't touch the victim directly.
3. Use a wooden object if needed.
4. Call 108 immediately.

📞 Emergency Numbers

🚑 Ambulance : 108

👮 Police : 100

🔥 Fire : 101

🚨 Emergency : 112
